Ducks

Sun, Apr 26, 2020

Using Van Aken Claytoon modeling clay. Making these ducks turned my hands yellow. Playing with Entangle to set up the shot. The shots for the animation had these settings:
Exposure Time 1 second
Aperture value f/5.0
ISO Speed Rating 100
Metering Mode Pattern
Focal Length 39.0 mm

There are 14 pictures in the animation. I used 10 frames frames per second to make ducks.

ffmpeg -r 10 -f image2 -s 1920x1080 -start_number  18 -i film%06d.jpg -vcodec libx264 -crf 25  -pix_fmt yuv420p ducks.mp4

Here is the result

Playing around with the frames per second. Here is 20 frames per second

The left duck moves out of focus as it moves towards the camera. Refocusing is something I am going to have to think about when the characters move towards or away from the camera.
