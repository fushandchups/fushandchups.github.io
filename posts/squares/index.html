Dancing Squares

Sun, Mar 14, 2021

Loop of hand drawn squares

10 frames per second.

ffmpeg -r 10 -f image2 -start_number 4 -s 1920x1080  -i film%06d.jpg -vcodec libx264 -crf 18  -pix_fmt yuv420p squares.mp4

Changing cfr

 ffmpeg -r 10 -f image2 -start_number 4 -s 1920x1080  -i film%06d.jpg -vcodec libx264 -crf 25  -pix_fmt yuv420p squares_crf25.mp4

Looping:

ffmpeg -i squares_crf.mp4 -filter_complex "loop=loop=10:size=20:start=0" -pix_fmt yuv420p squares_loop2.mp4
